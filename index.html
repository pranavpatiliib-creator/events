About SAFE

The Student Association of First Year Engineering (SAFE) at Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil College of Engineering serves as the foundational platform for aspiring engineers to cultivate technical excellence, creative innovation, and collaborative learning.


SPARKUP 2026 represents our commitment to fostering a culture of innovation and professional development. This prestigious event provides students with an unparalleled opportunity to showcase their technical prowess, creative capabilities, and presentation skills in a competitive yet supportive environment.

Rules & Guidelines

PPT Presentation - Rules

  • Maximum 10 slides only (including introduction and conclusion).
  • Presentation time: 4 minutes maximum.
  • Team size: 1-4 members.
  • Topic must relate to the event theme.
  • Format: PPT/PDF only.
  • Content must be original. No plagiarism allowed.
  • AI tools allowed only for minor help (grammar, structuring).
  • Exceeding slide or time limit will lead to disqualification.
  • Late submissions will not be accepted.

Project Making - Rules

  • Team size: 1-4 members.
  • Project must be innovative and original.
  • Working model is preferred.
  • Explanation time: 5 minutes.
  • AI tools allowed only for research assistance.
  • No copied or previously presented projects.
  • Judges' decision will be final.

Short Film Making - Rules

  • Theme: "Unspoken" (emotions or struggles not expressed in words; related to student life or social reality).
  • Duration: 6-9 minutes (including credits).
  • Team size: 1-4 members (registered students only).
  • Format: MP4, minimum 1080p, landscape mode only.
  • Content must be original. No plagiarism.
  • No copyrighted music without permission.
  • Any language allowed; subtitles mandatory if not English.
  • AI tools allowed only for minor enhancements (not full film generation).
  • Exceeding time limit, offensive content, plagiarism, or violation of theme will lead to disqualification.
  • Submit before deadline; late entries will not be accepted.

Reel Making - Rules

  • Theme must relate to the competition theme.
  • Duration: 30-60 seconds only.
  • Format: Vertical (9:16), MP4, minimum 1080p.
  • Team size: 1-4 members.
  • Content must be original. No copied or reposted reels.
  • Copyright-free music only.
  • Any language allowed; strictly no vulgar or offensive content.
  • Basic editing allowed; avoid excessive effects.
  • AI tools allowed only for minor enhancements (not full reel generation).
  • Exceeding time limit, plagiarism, inappropriate content, or violation of theme will lead to disqualification.
  • Late submissions will not be accepted.

Cultural Event - Rules

  • Categories: Singing, Dancing, Poetry, Stand-up Comedy, and Skit.
  • Team size: 1-4 members (solo or group as per category).
  • Performance time: 3-5 minutes per entry.
  • Content must be original, respectful, and suitable for a college audience.
  • No vulgar, offensive, or discriminatory content allowed.
  • Use of copyrighted tracks is participant responsibility; prefer royalty-free audio.
  • AI tools allowed only for minor support (script polishing, background planning), not full content generation.
  • Judging criteria: creativity, expression, stage presence, and audience engagement.
  • Exceeding time limit, plagiarism, or inappropriate content may lead to disqualification.
  • Late registrations/submissions will not be accepted.
