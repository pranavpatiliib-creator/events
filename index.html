About SAFE

The Student Association of First Year Engineering (SAFE) at Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil College of Engineering serves as the foundational platform for aspiring engineers to cultivate technical excellence, creative innovation, and collaborative learning.


SPARKUP 2026 represents our commitment to fostering a culture of innovation and professional development. This prestigious event provides students with an unparalleled opportunity to showcase their technical prowess, creative capabilities, and presentation skills in a competitive yet supportive environment.
